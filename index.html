About Me

I am an incoming MSc Robotics student at the University of New South Wales. Over the past three years, I have participated in research projects, particularly working on the development related to computer vision. I worked on the research project CHARM: Companion Healthcare Aid Robot Manager with Prof. Li-Chen Fu at National Taiwan University. I designed a user behaviour recognition system to detect abnormal behaviour of the elderly and reduce the risk of falls. The work has been awarded the 2021 National Innovation Award in Academic Research.
From these experiences, I have developed a passion for not only providing engineering solutions to various real-world problems but also being a contributor to novel robotics technologies.
